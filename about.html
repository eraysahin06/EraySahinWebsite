About me

Who am I?

After completing my secondary education in Ankara, Turkey, I came to Toronto, Canada to continue my education at Seneca College Applied Arts & Technology - Computer Programming. I am interested in game programming and completed some game projects including mobile and desktop games using the Unity Game Engine and C# programming language.


When I went to Turkey for a summer break, I worked at Netsinerji Bilişim as an SQL Programmer for a month and improved my work experience. Even though I didn't have enough time because I spent 2 months in Turkey and I was a seasonal employee, it was a great experience for me. Apart from the C and C++ languages ​​I learned at school, I continue to improve myself in C#, Java, and Python programming languages, and I will also attend the SDET (Software Development Engineer in Test) boot camp, a 6-month certificate program prepared by Techno Study, at the end of October 2022. During my secondary education, I attended many Model United Nations conferences including Bilkent University Historical MUN and I tried to take part in all possible voluntary events and activities.


There are also fields that I am interested in other than game and application development, such as motorsports and music production, and drawing. I have been granted a distinction award from the University of West London - College of Music Examinations - Electric Guitar Grade 1 Examination.
